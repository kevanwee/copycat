# Singapore Legal Research Mapping (sg_v1)

## Core Sources
- Singapore Copyright Act 2021 (2025 consolidation): https://www.wipo.int/wipolex/edocs/lexdocs/laws/en/sg/sg223en.pdf
- SGCA substantial part / copying guidance: https://www.elitigation.sg/gd/s/2017_SGCA_28
- SGCA originality reference: https://www.elitigation.sg/gd/s/2011_SGCA_37

## Triage Phases
1. Subsistence
- Protected work category
- Originality + fixation
- Singapore qualification/connection
- Term/status plausibility

2. Infringement
- Ownership/standing asserted
- Acts map to rights
- Lack of authorization/license
- Copying indicators: causal access + objective similarity

3. Substantial Taking
- Quality/importance emphasis (not quantity alone)

4. Exceptions/Defences
- Fair use and statutory exception indicators as risk modifiers

## Output Character
- Triage signal only.
- Does not output a legal conclusion.
- Explicit disclaimers in report.
